Common Issues

No Sound

  • Check your device volume and mute switch
  • Make sure sound effects are enabled in the app settings

Ads Not Showing

  • Ads may not appear every time
  • Availability depends on network conditions and family settings

Gameplay Issues

  • Restart the app
  • Make sure your device is running the latest iOS version

Bug Reports

When reporting a bug, please include:

  • Device model (e.g., iPhone 15, iPad 13")
  • iOS version
  • Description of what happened
  • Screenshot or screen recording if possible
